#### ArrayBlockingQueue

* 队列: 数组
* 线程安全: ReentrantLock
* 阻塞: Condition

#### LinkedBlockingQueue

* 队列: 单向链表
* 线程安全: ReentrantLock
* 阻塞: Condition

#### PriorityBlockingQueue

* 优先级队列: 最小堆
* 线程安全: ReentrantLock
* 阻塞: Condition

#### DelayQueue

* 延迟: PriorityQueue
* 线程安全: ReentrantLock
* 阻塞: Condition
